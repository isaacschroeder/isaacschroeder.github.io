A Little Bit About Me

Hello, I am Isaac Schroeder. I am a senior in computer science at the University of Missouri.
I have always enjoyed anything involving problem solving, so I was drawn to programming as a career. I have lived in Columbia Missouri my whole life, but I look forward to the possibility of traveling and learning about other places through a future career. In my free time, I enjoy gaming, both in video form and tabletop form. I find gaming as a great way to spend time with my friends, especially those who live far away. Additionally, I like to be outside whenever the weather is nice. You will often find me out on a local disc golf course. Disc golfing has recently become my favorite outdoor activity. I like playing casual disc golf rounds with my friends, and I have begun competing in tournaments as well.

Education

Currently pursuing a bachelors in computer science at the University of Missouri, Columbia. Additionally, completing a minor in mathematics. On track to complete education by May 2023.
